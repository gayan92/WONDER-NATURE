ADAM'S PEAK(SRI PADAYA)

Adam's Peak is a 2,243 m (7,359 ft) tall conical mountain located in central of
Sri Lanka
It is well known for the Sri Pada rock formation near the summit, which in Buddhist tradition is held to be the footprint of the Buddha, in Hindu tradition that of Hanuman or Shiva and in some Islamic and Christian traditions that of Adam, or that of St. Thomas
